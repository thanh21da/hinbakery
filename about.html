about us

what makes our cake shop special?

Hinbakery strives to operate our business in as ethical a manner as possible and place great emphasis on our legacy to future generations. We source local ingredients that are grown in systems which benefit the ecological health of the planet, and we work with farmers who share our vision of an Agrarian Renaissance. Making food that is healthy, for ourselves and for the planet, and sharing this process in an open manner with as many people as possible are key aspirations of our company.

We would like to thank all the photographers and illustrators that have supplied us with beautiful images over the years. Thank you to Analisa Fernandes, Helen Cathcart, Andy Sewell, Kaan Diskaya, Florencia Lucila, Carlotta Marangone, Dan Cartwright and Sarah Bates.
